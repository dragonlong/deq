Virginia Department of Environmental Quality (VADEQ) has partnered with Advanced Research Computing (ARC) at Virginia Tech since 2011. ARC provides computing resources for DEQ staff to conduct emission and air quality modeling. Below are sample work completed by DEQ and presented at various conference calls and meetings over the years. Feel free to download the presentations or view them online.
